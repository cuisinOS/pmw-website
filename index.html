Featured Strategic Engagement

Turkish Football Federation (TFF)

PMW operates as the licensed merchandise and brand systems partner for TFF — managing the full product lifecycle from concept to shelf across national team and institutional verticals.

  • 35+ SKUs designed, produced, and distributed under official TFF license
  • Seasonal drop architecture aligned with national team calendar and tournament cycles
  • EURO 2024 capsule collection — designed, produced, and launched within PMW infrastructure
  • Multi-channel distribution: e-commerce, stadium retail, wholesale, and institutional gifting
